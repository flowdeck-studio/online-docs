---
title: 'Asset Catalog Explorer'
description: 'Explore Asset Catalogs from Cursor'
---

FlowDeck includes an Asset Catalog Browser, that allows you to open and explore .xcasset catalogs.

<Info>
  Asset Catalog Browser is Read-only. Editing functionality coming soon
</Info>

It supports assets of the following types:

- Folders & Nested Folders
- Images (PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF)
- Color
- Appearances & Variations (Universal, Light Dark)

To open an Asset Catalog, right-click on the folder and select "Open Asset Catalog" from the menu.
